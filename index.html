About Sleep Debt

Sleep debt accumulates when you consistently miss your optimal sleep hours. It disrupts brain function, mood regulation, and physical health over time.

  • Cognitive Impact: Impaired memory and decision-making.
  • Emotional Health: Increased stress, anxiety.
  • Physical Well-being: Weakened immunity, metabolic issues.
  • Hormonal Balance: Disrupted appetite hormones.
  • Performance: Lower productivity and focus.
